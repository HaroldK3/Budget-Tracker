Minimum Viable Product
Our budget tracker will at least allow users to log in and create accounts, enter their monthly earnings, spendings, and any saving goals for calculation. 

Basic Needs:

Needs expense/earned tracking function
Including Total Money = Earned - Expense
Budget Estimation
Based on earned/spent from last month?
Recurring payment tracker/reminder
Log in function - per teacher, cant avoid 🙃
Goals
Savings, Big purchases, etc. 
Spending Categories 
Some basics (rent,  utilities, food) and make your own

Not so basic needs:

Visualization of the expenses in a visually pleasing way  (pie chart like rocket money etc - minimalistic graphics)
Mayhaps even customizable
Financial tips
Overspending alerts
